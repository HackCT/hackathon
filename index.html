Hackathon

It is with great excitement that MakerspaceCT, Spark Makerspace, MakeHaven, Fairfield County Makers Guild, and Danbury Hackerspace accounce HackCT, a three-day hackathon event that will take place March 27th-29th, 2020 at MakerspaceCT in Hartford, CT.

Applications are open to the maker, hacker, and hardware/software development community at large, and can be found here.

Theme
The theme for HackCT is “smart, safe, and accessible cities”. Participants will brainstorm about the problems faced by modern city residents, commuters, and workers and will then break into groups to develop solutions to some of the problems. The theme was specifically chosen to promote the use of Internet of Things (IoT) devices, while still providing enough flexibility to ensure that participants can work on software of web applications if they prefer.
